Frequently Asked Questions

What size fountain do I need for my pond?

The ideal fountain size depends on your pond's dimensions. Generally, choose a fountain that circulates the pond's volume every 1-2 hours.

How often should I maintain my water feature?

Regular maintenance should be performed monthly, with deeper cleaning quarterly. Winter preparation is essential in colder climates.

What's the best way to prevent algae growth?

Maintain proper water circulation, use appropriate water treatments, and ensure balanced sunlight exposure to minimize algae growth.
