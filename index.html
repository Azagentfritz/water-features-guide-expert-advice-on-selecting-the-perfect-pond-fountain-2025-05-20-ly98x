Frequently Asked Questions

What size fountain do I need for my pond?

The ideal fountain size depends on your pond's dimensions and desired effect. For optimal results, choose a fountain that can circulate the pond's entire volume every 1-2 hours. A general rule is to select a fountain with a GPH (Gallons Per Hour) rating that's at least equal to your pond's volume.

How much does professional installation typically cost?

Professional installation costs typically range from $500-$2,500, depending on your location, fountain size, and site preparation needs. This includes electrical work, plumbing, and proper positioning for optimal display.

What maintenance is required throughout the year?

Regular maintenance includes monthly cleaning of filters, quarterly deep cleaning, and seasonal preparations. Winter requires special attention in colder climates, including proper drainage and storage of pump components.

How can I prevent algae growth in my fountain?

Prevent algae by maintaining proper water circulation, using appropriate water treatments, and ensuring balanced sunlight exposure. Regular cleaning and water testing are essential for long-term prevention.

What's the average lifespan of a quality pond fountain?

A high-quality pond fountain, with proper maintenance, typically lasts 10-15 years. The pump may need replacement every 3-5 years, depending on usage and water conditions.
